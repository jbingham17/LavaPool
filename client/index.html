Diversify Your Stake

Lava Pool provides a simple way to diversify your staking risk across multiple pooled staking platforms. In exchange for ETH, you will receive a LavaETH token that represents the rewards that your ETH earns through staking. LavaETH can then be returned at any time for your ETH, plus the staking rewards you earned. Currently Lava Pool uses an equal weighting of four of the largest liquidity pools, shown here.

Get Started Staking with Lava Pool

1. Access your MetaMask wallet and select "Send" at the top of the screen.

2. Enter the Lava Pool token address and enter the amount of ETH to stake:

0xcF0Eb69FD014216707DDB344D95Ba22011EB689A

3. Complete the transaction by selecting "Confirm". LavaETH will be added to your wallet in the amount corresponding to your staked ETH.

4. Return your LavaETH token at any time to re-claim your ETH and staking rewards!
